The 3 C's of the Ecosystem

Everything we build for entrepreneurs sits on three pillars. The back office is how members access all three.

Capital

Direct access to AmPac's full lending stack — SBA 504, SBA 7(a) Community Advantage, microloans, CDFI lending, Down Payment Assistance, Veteran Advantage, and three active municipal revolving loan funds.

$215.5M project financing in 2025 alone

Coaching

Two flagship incubator programs — LEAP 2 Launch and the City of Ontario Executive Incubator — plus 24/7 AI advisors, cohort-based curriculum, BBO workbooks, and direct 1:1 mentorship from AmPac's team.

17 LEAP graduates · 10 Ontario graduates in 2025

Community

A 3,085-member ecosystem with 51% women, 42.3% Black/African American, and 24.1% Hispanic/Latino representation — intentionally built to serve entrepreneurs most often left out of traditional capital and support networks.

CA · AZ · NV · 22+ Congressional districts

Business Plan & Projections Template

A 12-month financial projections template plus an EOS Vision/Traction Organizer to lock in your 1, 3, and 10-year plan.

Loan Readiness Self-Assessment

Evaluate your business against AmPac's underwriting criteria for SBA 504, 7(a), and microloan products before you apply.

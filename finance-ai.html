I just started my LLC. What accounting software should I use and how do I set up my chart of accounts?
Great question! For a new LLC, I recommend starting with QuickBooks Online Simple Start ($30/mo). Here is your essential chart of accounts:

Assets: Business Checking, Savings, Accounts Receivable
Liabilities: Credit Cards, Accounts Payable, Loans
Income: Service Revenue, Product Sales
Expenses: Rent, Utilities, Marketing, Payroll

Want me to walk you through setting this up step by step?
Yes! And can you help me understand my DSCR for an SBA loan?
Absolutely! Your Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) = Net Operating Income / Total Debt Service. Lenders typically want 1.25x or higher for SBA 7(a) loans. Send me your monthly revenue and expenses and I will calculate yours instantly.

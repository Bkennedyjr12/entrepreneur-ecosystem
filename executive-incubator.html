The Two Tracks

One Program. Two Pathways.

Leap 2 Launch meets entrepreneurs where they are — whether you're building your foundation or ready to scale.

Track 1

The Leap Program

For early-stage entrepreneurs building their business foundation. Pre-revenue, newly launched, or in your first few years — the Leap Program gives you structure, coaching, and capital connections to move forward with confidence.

  • 12-month structured cohort experience
  • Business planning, marketing, operations & financial training
  • Peer cohort accountability and expert mentorship
  • Formal 30-point judged pitch graduation
  • Access to the 3,000+ member Entrepreneur Ecosystem upon graduation

Participation Model

$1,000 deposit to join

At graduation, you pitch to a panel of judges and compete for a grant award.

Apply — Leap Program
Track 2

The Launch Program

For seasoned, scale-stage businesses ready to grow aggressively. You have revenue, a proven concept, and you're ready for executive-level coaching and serious capital access.

  • 12-month executive coaching group format
  • Strategic growth, capital readiness & operational systems
  • Executive peer group with accountability structure
  • Direct introduction to AmPac's capital network and lending programs
  • Qualify for SBA loans, CDFI lending, or investor capital at completion

Participation Model

Monthly membership fee

At completion, qualify for a loan or investment from a traditional lender or capital partner.

Our Framework

Built on the 3 C's

Capital. Coaching. Community. The foundation of everything AmPac builds — applied as a 12-month structured transformation.

Capital

Direct access to SBA 504, 7(a) Community Advantage, microloans, CDFI lending, Down Payment Assistance, Veteran Advantage, and state guaranty programs. Participants leave with real capital relationships — not just knowledge about them.

Coaching

Facilitated curriculum including the Building Business Owners (BBO) workbook, EOS Vision/Traction Organizer, financial management frameworks, and the Entrepreneurial Leap 1-2-3 Roadmap — covering business planning, finance, marketing, operations, and strategic growth.

Community

Every graduate enters the 3,000+ member Entrepreneur Ecosystem. Inaugural alumni return as mentors for incoming cohorts — creating a living, growing network where experience flows forward continuously.

What Happens Over 12 Months

Q1

Foundation

Business model clarity, market positioning, competitive analysis, and value proposition development.

Q2

Operations

Financial management, bookkeeping, cash flow, P&L, and operational systems that scale.

Q3

Growth

Q4

Capital & Pitch

Capital readiness, pitch preparation, and a formal 30-point judged pitch graduation ceremony.

Pitch Graduation

You Don't Just Complete the Program.
You Earn Your Graduation.

Every Leap participant graduates by delivering a 3-minute business pitch, scored by a panel of judges on a 30-point rubric. Real accountability for real results.

10 pts

Quality of Pitch

Scored "unclear" (0–3) to "compelling" (9–10). Tests how effectively you communicate your business in 3 minutes.

10 pts

Business Structure & KPIs

Scored "not identified" (0–3) to "actively used" (9–10). Tests whether you run your business by metrics.

10 pts

Growth & Program Impact

Scored "minimal" (0–3) to "significant" (9–10). Tests the measurable lift produced during your 12 months.

The 2023–2024 class was the first-ever cohort of the Leap 2 Launch Entrepreneur Development Program — and they ran an extended 18-month pilot format. Ten entrepreneurs committed to the full journey, putting in the work to build, validate, and strengthen their businesses under AmPac's Capital, Coaching & Community framework.

This inaugural class established the proof of concept. The insights from their 18 months directly shaped the refined 12-month curriculum now used in every cohort that follows. Their participation — and their businesses — are the foundation of this program's track record.

The 18-Month Journey

Months 1–4 · Foundation

Business model clarity, market analysis, value proposition, and initial financial baseline. Goals set for the full program.

Months 5–9 · Operations & Finance

Financial management, bookkeeping, cash flow, 12-month projections, and capital readiness work begins.

Months 10–14 · Marketing & Growth

Brand development, customer acquisition strategy, and 1-3-10 year vision using the EOS Traction Organizer.

Months 15–18 · Capital & Graduation

Capital request development, lender introductions, and program graduation. Learnings from this phase informed the formal 30-point pitch rubric introduced in Cohort 2.

Executive Incubator

A Standalone Program for Cities, Counties & Organizations

The Executive Incubator is AmPac's premium, fully customized model — built on the Leap 2 Launch framework but deployed as a standalone program for municipalities, economic development agencies, and organizations that want to invest in their business community at a higher level.

What Makes It Different

Higher-Level Program Design

Built on the Leap 2 Launch model but elevated — deeper curriculum, more intensive coaching, and customized deliverables built around your community's specific needs.

12-Month Structured Format

The proven 12-month framework, adapted to your organization's timeline, cohort size, and program goals — with AmPac managing curriculum and delivery end to end.

Measurable Outcomes & Reporting

Comprehensive reporting on participant growth, capital incubated, pitch performance, and community impact — giving funders and stakeholders clear, defensible ROI.

Turnkey Partnership with AmPac

AmPac handles program design, curriculum delivery, mentorship, and capital connectivity. You bring the community and the mission — we bring the model.

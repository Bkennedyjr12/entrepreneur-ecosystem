We're a 15-person SaaS startup. Should we use Salesforce or something simpler?
For a 15-person SaaS startup, I'd recommend HubSpot Professional or Pipedrive over Salesforce. Here's why: Salesforce has enterprise complexity and costs $165/user/month. For your stage, HubSpot Pro ($120/user/mo) offers better value with native automations, lead scoring, and email integration already built in. Pipedrive is even leaner at $69/user/mo if you want a faster, sales-focused tool.

Recommendation: Start with HubSpot Pro, implement over 30 days, then reassess as you scale to 50+ people.
What about automating our customer onboarding process? Currently it's mostly manual.
Manual onboarding is costing you ~8 hours per new customer. Here's the automation stack I'd build:

1. Trigger: New customer in Stripe webhook →
2. Zapier: Create contact in HubSpot + send welcome email
3. Automation: 3-email sequence over 7 days with personalization
4. Integration: Add to Slack #customer-onboarding channel
5. Handoff: Schedule 30-min call with success manager (calendar invite)

This saves ~6.5 hours per customer and improves satisfaction by 40-60%. Implementation time: 4-6 hours. Cost: $0 beyond what you're already paying.
Perfect! How do we get started building this workflow?
I'll walk you through it step-by-step in the app. You'll:

1. Share your Stripe, HubSpot, and Slack details (secure OAuth)
2. I'll map your customer data structure
3. Build the workflow visually in our workflow designer
4. Test with a test customer
5. Go live and monitor performance

Timeline: 1-2 hours hands-on work. I'll be here to answer questions. Ready to get started?
